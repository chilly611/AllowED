How It Works

From enrollment
to VA submission
in seconds.

AllowED connects to your Student Information System, applies VA rules automatically, and submits certifications through the VA API. Here's the flow:

1

Pull enrollment data

AllowED reads directly from PeopleSoft, Ellucian, or your SIS.

2

Run decision tree

7-step rule engine evaluates every course: audit filter, repeat check, modality, training time, RoP.

3

Match to WEAMS

3-tier matching (exact, structured, fuzzy) confirms the student's program is VA-approved.

4

Certify or flag

Clean certifications auto-process. Anything risky goes to the SCO review queue.

1

Snapshot comparison

AllowED takes enrollment snapshots and detects adds, drops, withdrawals, and unit changes.

2

Academic calendar aware

Knows your add/drop deadline, census date, and withdrawal deadline. Generates the right amendment type.

3

Program change detection

Catches major changes and triggers WEAMS re-match. Always flags for SCO review.

4

Graduation tracking

Detects degree conferral, generates graduated amendment, verifies final-term rounding out eligibility.

1

Generate EM fields

Decision tree output maps to every Enrollment Manager field. Credits, tuition, training time, chapter.

2

Create certification PDF

Professional PDF with student info, course detail, certification statement. Audit-ready.

3

Submit via VA API

Upload to VA Benefits Intake API. Track status in real-time. Rate limits handled automatically.

4

Confirm & log

Submission confirmed. GUID tracked. Full audit trail preserved for every action.
